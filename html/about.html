About Us

At QuestQuest, we believe travel is more than just seeing new places—it’s about finding unique experiences that spark joy, curiosity, and adventure. Our mission is to take the guesswork out of planning, so you can dive straight into unforgettable journeys. With a team of passionate explorers and local insiders, QuestQuest curates personalized recommendations, hidden gems, and immersive itineraries tailored just for you.

Whether you’re seeking a culinary escape, a nature adventure, or a deep dive into culture, we’re here to guide you every step of the way. Let QuestQuest help you turn your next trip into an adventure worth remembering—one recommendation at a time. Discover your world with us!


Olivia Chen – Chief Adventure Officer

Olivia is the driving force behind QuestQuest’s mission to redefine travel recommendations. With over a decade of experience in experiential travel, she has explored more than 50 countries and lived in 5. Her knack for uncovering hidden gems and her genuine passion for connecting with local cultures make her a true adventurer at heart. When she's not leading the team, Olivia is likely hiking a remote trail or trying out street food in an undiscovered corner of the world.


Jasper Reyes – Destination Curator

Jasper brings an insider's edge to QuestQuest’s itineraries, focusing on unique, immersive experiences. With a background in anthropology and sustainable tourism, he specializes in crafting eco-friendly trips that highlight local traditions and landscapes. Known for his storytelling flair, Jasper curates recommendations that let travelers see destinations through the eyes of the people who live there. A natural explorer, he’s always on the lookout for the next adventure that inspires authentic connections and meaningful memories.


Mira Patel – Head of Customer Magic

Mira is the heart and soul of QuestQuest’s customer experience. With a background in hospitality and years of expertise in making people feel at home around the world, she ensures that every QuestQuest traveler feels supported, from the planning phase to post-trip reflections. Her commitment to exceptional service and her love for exploring with her family have fueled her passion for helping others discover new places comfortably. Mira’s goal? To make every journey a magical and seamless adventure.
